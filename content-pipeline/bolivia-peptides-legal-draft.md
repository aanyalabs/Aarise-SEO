## Are Peptides Legal in Bolivia? Complete AGEMED Guide for 2026

Research peptides occupy a specific regulatory space in Bolivia. Understanding the rules set by **AGEMED** (Agencia de Medicamentos y Tecnologías en Salud) is essential before importing or purchasing peptides for research purposes.

### Quick Answer

**Yes, research peptides are legal in Bolivia under specific conditions.** Peptides classified as Research Use Only (RUO) are not regulated as pharmaceutical drugs by AGEMED when used for laboratory research. However, peptides intended for human use require full pharmaceutical registration.

---

### What is AGEMED?

AGEMED is Bolivia's national regulatory authority for medicines and health technologies, operating under the Ministry of Health. It controls the import, manufacture, distribution, and sale of pharmaceutical products in Bolivia.

AGEMED follows the MERCOSUR regulatory framework (Bolivia is an associate member), which means its standards align broadly with Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Uruguay.
