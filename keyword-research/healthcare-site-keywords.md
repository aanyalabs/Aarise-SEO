## Key Finding: Wrong Keyword Strategy

**Original assumption:** Target "buy research peptides [country]" keywords.  
**Reality check from Keyword Planner:** These keywords get 0-10 searches/month globally.  
B2B research compound buyers do NOT use Google to find suppliers — they use Pharmacompass, TradeIndia, and LinkedIn.

**Pivot:** Target regulatory/legal intent keywords — people searching "are peptides legal in X?" BEFORE they buy. This is the actual top-of-funnel.
