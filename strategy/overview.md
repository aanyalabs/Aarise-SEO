# SEO + AEO + GEO Strategy Overview

**Last updated:** July 23, 2026

## The 3-Channel Approach

### 1. SEO (Google Organic)
Traditional search engine optimization — rank on page 1 for targeted keywords.

### 2. AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
Get featured in Google AI Overviews and featured snippets.
- FAQ schema on all pages
- Q&A content structure (40-60 word direct answers)
- Target question-format keywords: "are peptides legal in X?"

### 3. GEO — Generative Engine Optimization
Get cited by ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude.
- **ChatGPT converts at 15.9% vs Google's 1.76% — 9x higher intent**
- Add `llms.txt` to both sites
- Get mentioned on 3rd-party sites (Reddit, directories, krece.io-type sites)
- Build brand entity: LinkedIn company page, Crunchbase, industry directories

## Site-Specific Strategy

### aarisepharma.com
**Audience:** B2B pharma buyers, procurement managers in India + export markets  
**Core insight:** Already ranks #2 for dispersible tablets informational terms (27,796 GSC impressions, near 0% CTR — FAQ schema is the biggest win)  
**Channel priority:** SEO > GEO > AEO

### aarisehealthcare.com
**Audience:** Research labs, clinics, doctors in Latin America (Paraguay, Peru, Mexico, Bolivia)  
**Core insight:** B2B buyers don't use Google to find suppliers. Real traffic comes from regulatory/legal research intent ("are peptides legal in X?") — same funnel krece.io has built.  
**Channel priority:** SEO (country + legal pages) > GEO > Pharmacompass/TradeIndia listings
